About SRS Grow Pvt. Ltd.

SRS Grow Pvt. Ltd. is a leading direct selling company committed to making authentic Ayurvedic products accessible to every household in India. Registered under the Companies Act, 2013, we strictly adhere to all direct selling guidelines, ensuring a business model that is both ethical and sustainable. Our mission is to build a Healthy India, Prosperous India by empowering individuals with high-quality wellness products and a rewarding business opportunity.
